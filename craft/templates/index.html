What will the museum be?

The Women’s Suffrage Museum’s mission is to portray women’s struggle for equal rights and the opportunity for a vote in our democracy.

The site is considered hallowed ground

for the suffragists and for the modern women’s movement.

It will provide historical context to the movement toward franchise and also provide individual stories of the women

who helped bring about the ratification of the 19th Amendment to the U.S. Constitution.
